Our Journey

2015

Founded in a Small Studio

Elegance Fashion began as a passion project in a tiny Brooklyn studio, with just three team members and a dream to redefine fashion retail.

2017

First Retail Store

Opened our flagship store in SoHo, New York, marking our transition from online-only to a physical retail presence.

2019

Sustainability Initiative

Launched our eco-friendly collection and committed to carbon-neutral shipping, reducing our environmental impact by 40%.

2022

Global Expansion

Expanded to serve customers in over 50 countries worldwide, with localized websites and shipping centers.

2023

1 Million Customers

Celebrated serving our one millionth customer while maintaining a 98% satisfaction rate.
